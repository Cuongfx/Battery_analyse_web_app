# README

This dataset consists of 40 commercial Li-ion batteries in a format of pouch whose positive electrode is NMC111, the negative electrode is graphite, and the electrolyte consists of 1.0 M LiPF6 salt in solvents of EC and EMC with a ratio of 3:7 together with 2 wt% additive of vinylene carbonate. The nominal capacity of all batteries is 2.36 Ah.  There are 2 different protocols with 2 different working temperatures (25°C and 45°C).



If you use this dataset, please cite the original papers:

- **Predicting the impact of formation protocols on battery lifetime immediately after manufacturing** *2021 Joule*
