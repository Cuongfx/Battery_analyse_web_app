# README

This dataset consists of 12 commercial Li-ion batteries in pouch format whose positive electrode is NMC111, the negative electrode is graphite, and the electrolyte consists of 1M LiPF6 salt in solvents of ethylene carbonate (EC) and ethyl methyl carbonate (EMC) with a ratio of 3:7. The nominal capacity of all batteries is 5.0 Ah. There are 6 different protocols with 3 different working temperatures in total (-5°C, 25°C, and 45°C).



If you use this dataset, please cite the original papers:

- **Predicting the impact of formation protocols on battery lifetime immediately after manufacturing** *2021 Joule*
